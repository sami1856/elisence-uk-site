Who is Sami Salami?

Profile

Sami Salami is a UK-based founder working at the intersection of digital health, regulation, and human-centred technology. His work on Elisence focuses on long-term, preventive health – not short-term apps or attention-grabbing products.

Role: Founder and chief architect of the Elisence platform.
Focus: Safe, explainable and regulator-ready health-AI.
Regions: UK, Europe, and Gulf countries.

Work on Elisence

Platform

Elisence is being designed as a long-term health and humanity platform – combining lifestyle tracking, clinical-grade safety rules, and ministry-level reporting into one coherent ecosystem.

Alignment: Inspired by NHS clinical safety thinking (DCB 0129/0160).
Scope: From metabolic and GLP-1 support to women’s health, nutrition, and mental wellbeing.
Goal: Give families and health systems a calmer, clearer and more dignified digital experience.

Approach & values

Principles

The approach behind Elisence is deliberately cautious: non-diagnostic, non-prescriptive, and deeply respectful of clinical standards, privacy and legal boundaries in each country.

Human first: Technology should support real life, not replace it.
Evidence-led: Designs are built to be reviewable by clinicians, regulators and independent experts.
Calm by design: No addictive mechanics – the platform is built around healthy dopamine, not endless scrolling.
